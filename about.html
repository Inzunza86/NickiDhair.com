About Me

My name is Nicki Green DeHaven. I was born in Kansas City and currently live in Gardner. In 2001, I started my journey in the hair industry. I currently have a booth at The Hairem in Olathe. Since 2001, I have been expanding my skills in color application, hairstyles, nail techniques, and many other skills. I have had opportunity to go to hair shows and learn from the best in the business. I have certifications in Redken Exchange, Tressallure, extensions, Keratin treatments. I truly love what I do and enjoy watching my clients smile when they look into the mirror.
